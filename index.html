Talkhouse

Blending head-slamming highs and tear-jerking lows, TALKHOUSE offers carefully crafted lyrics, melodies, and riffs for listeners who enjoy complexity but still know when to let go and just have fun.

A product of New York, TALKHOUSE has been tearing up stages like Arlene's Grocery, selling out the Bitter End, and headlining Mercury Lounge and more.
